Activities at Pacific Trails

Hiking

Pacific Trails Resort as 5 miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to a state park. Go it alone or join on e of our guided hikes.

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are available for guest use.

Bird Watching

While anytime is a good time for bird watching at Pacific Trails, we offer guided bird watching trips at sunrise several times a week.
